# Pro-Level Prompt for AI Level Background Generation

**Task**

Transform the provided level layout image into a **2D pixel-art cave mineshaft environment** while **strictly preserving the level geometry** so the result remains usable as a game level background.

---

# 1. Layout Preservation (CRITICAL)

The original image defines the **gameplay geometry** and must remain unchanged.

Rules:

• **Do NOT move platforms**
• **Do NOT change the grid structure**
• **Do NOT extend platforms into empty space**
• **Do NOT alter the position of any platform**

The **exact horizontal top edge of every platform must remain identical to the source image.**

This ensures the level stays playable.

---

## Allowed Platform Modifications

The following modifications are allowed to improve realism:

• Lower edges of platforms can be **irregular and rocky**
• Vertical edges can be **rough and chipped**
• Platforms may appear **slightly narrower beneath the top edge**
• Undersides may include **broken rock layers and natural erosion**

However:

• The **top collision surface must remain perfectly unchanged**
• The **corner where the top edge meets the vertical edge must not move**

Think of it as **sculpting the underside of the rock**, not moving the platform.

---

# 2. Lighting Preservation

The **brightness pattern of the original image must remain intact**.

Rules:

• Dark areas remain dark
• Light areas remain light
• Maintain the same contrast structure
• Do not relocate light sources

Lighting style:

• Warm **lantern illumination**
• Subtle orange **amber glow**
• Deep cave shadows
• Soft falloff around lamps

The original brightness map acts as a **lighting guide**.

---

# 3. Visual Style — Underground Mine

Convert the environment into a **realistic underground mine shaft**.

### Platforms

Platforms should look like:

• Layered natural rock
• Rough fractured stone
• Slight cracks and mineral variations
• Broken rock edges

Avoid:

• Smooth cartoon stone
• Rounded shapes
• Plastic-looking rock

---

### Background (Important for Depth)

Create **multiple depth layers** in the cave wall.

Background should include:

• Dark distant cave walls
• Subtle rock formations
• Recessed cavern pockets
• Layered stone silhouettes
• Slight atmospheric fading for depth

Background must remain **less detailed than the platforms** to preserve readability.

---

### Environmental Storytelling

Add subtle mine details:

• Hanging lanterns
• Chains and ropes
• Wooden support beams
• Mine ladders
• Crates and barrels
• Wooden planks
• Broken mine rails
• Rock debris

These elements must **not obstruct gameplay paths**.

---

# 4. Pixel Art Constraints (Important)

The output must follow strict pixel art rules.

Palette:

• Maximum **64 colors**
• Indexed palette
• No dithering
• Warm mine color scheme

Recommended palette groups:

• Dark cave browns
• Rock grays
• Amber lantern glow
• Wood tones
• Shadow colors

---

# 5. Tile Safety

Ensure the level is **safe for game tiling systems**.

Rules:

• Avoid noisy textures
• Avoid single-pixel color noise
• Cluster shading into readable groups
• Avoid gradient banding

Textures should look **clean and structured**.

---

# 6. Gameplay Readability

Maintain strong platform clarity.

Rules:

• Platform tops must be visually distinct
• Edges clearly readable
• Player path obvious
• Avoid clutter on the playable surfaces

Foreground = readable
Background = atmospheric

---

# 7. Final Export Requirements

Resolution:

**256 × 224 pixels**

Scaling:

• Nearest neighbor only
• Preserve pixel sharpness

Format:

• PNG
• Indexed palette
• Maximum 64 colors

---
